How does Miscommunication affects your daily life?
Communication is one of the most important factors of how we can interact with other people, the lack of communication can not only affect human relationships but also the self esteem,cooperation and the future life of the person, therefore it is important to recognise the problem of misscommunication and take action in order to achieve confidence in your communication skills
